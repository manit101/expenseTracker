About BudgetBuddy

Our Mission

We aim to provide users with a comprehensive solution for managing their finances. Our features include:

  • Expense Tracker: Monitor your spending and stay on budget.
  • QR Code Payments: Easily pay your taxes with a quick scan.
  • Tax Calculations: Calculate your taxes using both new and old tax slabs.

Join us on our journey to make financial management accessible and straightforward for everyone.
